The Foyer

A contemporary art practitioner interested in the intersection of production and curation. Exploring the architectural silence of the white cube through digital spatial experiences and institutional frameworks.

This exhibition maps a curatorial journey through institutional galleries, independent studios, and international biennales — from the Science Gallery Bengaluru to the Kochi-Muziris Biennale.
